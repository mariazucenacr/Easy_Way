Our Item

Tree Train Model EW-T4

Our item is a locomotive 3 wagons tree with support.

It can be easily assembled around the Christmas tree. The rail can fit the great majority of artificial and natural trees from 1.2 to 2.3 meters.

The parts included are:

  • 8 track sections
  • 8 support struts
  • 1 tree clamp
  • 2 stabilizers
  • 1 engine
  • 1 fuel car
  • 1 caboose

*Special Characteristics: Lights and sound.
